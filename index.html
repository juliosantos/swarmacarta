Swarma Carta

Swarma Carta, aside from bad and far-fetched wordplay, is a small web app you can use to plot your Foursquare/Swarm check-ins on a map, explore where you've been, and share it with others.

It's a tiny experiment by Júlio Santos. He needed an excuse to play with Parse.
